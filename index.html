About Us

With more than seven years of experience in the dental industry, our New York-based company is passionate about helping dental practices thrive. We provide individually tailored services, leveraging our expertise to ensure your practice receives the best possible support. 

Services

Dental Billing 

Dental billing involves submitting dental claims pre-estimates to insurance companies for payment, and meticulously tracking outstanding claims to ensure timely reimbursement for services rendered.

Dental credentialing

Dental credentialing verifies a provider's qualifications and credentials, involving initial applications to insurance companies and regular recredentialing to maintain network participation. It ensures providers meet specific standards for patient care and billing.

Production 

Dental doctor production, tailored to individual practice needs, measures the revenue generated by a dentist, helping practices optimize scheduling, treatment plans, and overall efficiency to maximize profitability.

Introducing PracticePulse:

Strategic Consulting for Dental Practice Evolution.

PracticePulse is our specialized consulting service dedicated to helping dental practices transition from standard clinical operations to becoming robust, well-managed dental facilities primed for growth. We understand the challenges beyond the chairside and focus on building a stronger business foundation for your practice.

Our Process:

Comprehensive Analysis: We conduct an in-depth review of your practice, identifying key operational strengths, hidden weaknesses, potential opportunities, and emerging threats.

Collaborative Discussion: We present our findings clearly and engage in open dialogue to ensure a shared understanding of your practice's current state and future potential.

Actionable Growth Project: Together, we translate insights into a tailored, strategic project plan with defined steps aimed at enhancing management, improving efficiency, and driving growth.

 

 

Flexible Access to Expertise:

Synergistic Value: PracticePulse is offered as a complimentary service for clients leveraging our comprehensive dental billing solutions, providing integrated financial and operational support.

Objective Standalone Assessment: Need a fresh perspective or an independent second opinion? PracticePulse is available as a separate, standalone service, offering unbiased analysis and strategic recommendations focused solely on your practice's growth and management needs.
